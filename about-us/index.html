What is FTC?

The FTC is an international robotics competition for students in grades 7 through 12 where teams design, build and program robots to compete in a themed game. It is organized by FIRST and emphasizes teamwork, innovation and problem solving. Participants use a combination of engineering, coding typically in Java and strategic thinking to complete tasks and earn points. Beyond the robot game the FTC also values community outreach, engineering documentation and gracious professionalism.
